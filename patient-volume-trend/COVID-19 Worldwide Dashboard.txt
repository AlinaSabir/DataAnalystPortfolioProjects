This repository contains a Power BI Dashboard that presents a comprehensive analysis of global COVID-19 data, offering insights into total cases, deaths, recoveries, active cases, and population impact across countries.

Dataset: 

The Covid_19 Dataset freely provided by the World in Data. The dataset is
produced by third parties and made available by World In Data.

Dashboard Highlights

It shows Sum of Active Cases by Country. A horizontal bar chart visualizing the number of active cases across countries, allowing for quick comparison.
Deaths vs Total Recovered and it Visualizes the relationship between total deaths and recovered cases, identifying trends and outliers. It also shows distribution of Total Deaths, Total Recovered, and Active Cases as a percentage of total reported cases.
